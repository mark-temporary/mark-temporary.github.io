### Welcome to the official home of Happy Ferret Entertainment

As of the time of writing, the website is still under heavy
development.

In the future, I hope to make this an excellent source for
information on all of our upcoming games.

<!-- truncate -->

The little game window you find as part of the landing page
represents an attempt at providing information interactively
and playfully.

While we'll always maintain a more orthodox way to view our website,
interested parties will be able to browse concept art, trailer videos,
reviews and a metric ton of other information from the comfort
of our in-house SCUNK engine (the same engine that will drive many of our future titles).
